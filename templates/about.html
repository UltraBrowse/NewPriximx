About Us

UltraBrowse was founded in March 2024 by Charmunk and Virtual. We started out hosting Kasm Workspaces instances for people to use. In April 2024, KAS joined UltraBrowse as a Developer, and eventually became a co-owner in late april. UltraBrowse then created its own web proxy. It had over 550 users while it was running. Some of UltraBrowse's other creations include: The UltraBrowse Discord bot, NewPrixmixManager and NewPrixmix.
